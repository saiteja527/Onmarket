FAQ's

What are the store's opening hours?

Our store is open from 8:00 AM to 10:00 PM, seven days a week.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept cash, credit cards, debit cards, mobile payments, and contactless payments like Apple Pay and Google Pay.

Are there any current promotions or discounts available?

Yes, we frequently have promotions and discounts on various products. Check our website or in-store displays for the latest offers.

Can I return or exchange products if I'm not satisfied?

Yes, we have a hassle-free return and exchange policy. Please keep your receipt and return the item within 30 days for a refund or exchange.

What is your policy on expired or damaged products?

We strive to provide fresh and high-quality products. If you find an expired or damaged item, please bring it to our attention, and we will gladly replace it or refund your purchase.

Do you carry organic or locally sourced products?

Absolutely! We offer a wide selection of organic and locally sourced products to support sustainable and local farming practices.

Are shopping carts and baskets available for customer use?

Yes, we provide shopping carts and baskets to make your shopping experience more convenient.

Do you offer assistance to customers with heavy or bulky items?

Yes, our staff is always ready to assist you with heavy or bulky items to your vehicle upon request.

Can I use reusable bags at the checkout?

Absolutely! We encourage customers to use reusable bags to reduce plastic waste. You can bring your own bags or purchase reusable bags at the store.
